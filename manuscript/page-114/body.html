OEDIPUS.

Saw'st ever there this man thou seëst now?

SHEPHERD.

There, Lord? What doing?—What man meanest thou?

OEDIPUS.

[Pointing to the STRANGER.

Look! Hath he ever crossed thy path before?

![Sidenote: vv. 1131-1146]
